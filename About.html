About Us :

Rikas Makeover is one the fastest growing Makeover brands in and around Madurai. Besides being Specialised in Bridal Makeup, we also offer bridesmaid makeup, Groom makeover, Hairstyling, Saree drapping, Mehandi and other parlour services as well. We are your one stop solution to make you look the best on your special occasion. Become a heroine with Rikas makeover. We have a parlour located at Kalavasal in Madurai. Our premium services include Hair Extensions, Airbrush Makeup, Hydrafacial etc. We are also experimenting with the tattoo services. We are open to travel all across the state to make you look beautiful. We know you are so beautiful by heart. We also thrive to enhance your facial beauty.

Meet the Founder

Rikas Makeover was founded in 2020 by the ever enthusiastic Makeup Artist Kausalya. She did not find her muse in the IT industry and decided to pursue her passion towards makeup and media in 2020. Though she had a quirky beginning, she has seen a tremendous growth after her marriage since she was enormously supported by her better half Mr.Sundar. Now Rikas Makeover is one of the growing brands in the Makeover niche and Kausalya has won the hearts of many local clients in n around Madurai. She is deeply passionated and is extremely skilled in matching her makeup with your skin tone. She is an international certified PRO Makeup Artist from Chennai who wants to make the people around the world look beautiful and their best on their special occasions.
